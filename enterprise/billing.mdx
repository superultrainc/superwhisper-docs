## Setting Up Billing

### Initial Billing Configuration

From your dashboard, select the **Billing** tab to set up your billing information and create your subscription.

![Billing Setup](/images/enterprise/billing_setup.png)

**Important:** Billing information is required in order to provision licenses to members.

### Seat-Based Pricing

Superwhisper Enterprise is seat-based:

- Choose **monthly** or **yearly** billing when creating your subscription
- You're charged for the number of seats purchased
- Purchase more seats to invite additional members
- Remove seats you aren't using

## Managing Your Subscription

![Subscription Management](/images/enterprise/billing_manage.png)

- View your current seat count and usage in the Billing tab
- Add or remove seats as your team size changes
- View billing history and upcoming charges in the Billing tab

## Seat Auto-Provisioning

Once your subscription is created, toggle **seat auto-provisioning** in the Billing tab. When enabled:

- New seats are added automatically when members try to join at capacity
- New team members can join without admin intervention
- No onboarding delays when seats run out

Useful for growing teams where manual seat management slows onboarding.

Without seat auto-provisioning, owners get an email when members try to log in without available seats, prompting them to purchase seats and re-invite the member.

## Keeping Costs Under Control

- **Wire up [SCIM deprovisioning](/enterprise/scim)** so departing employees are removed automatically and their seats freed
- **Quarterly seat audit**: review the Members tab and remove anyone who hasn't activated or hasn't used the app in 60+ days
- **Forecast before you add**: use the in-dashboard billing calculator before bulk-inviting a new team
- **Enable seat auto-provisioning** if you're scaling fast and don't want to gate hires on a manual seat purchase
