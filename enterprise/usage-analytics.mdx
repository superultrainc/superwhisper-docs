Usage analytics is an **optional** feature that gives Enterprise admins visibility into how their team is using Superwhisper. It's off by default. An Owner can turn it on from the dashboard.

![Usage Analytics Dashboard](/images/enterprise/usage_analytics_dashboard.png)

## What We Collect

When enabled, Superwhisper records aggregate, non-content usage events tied to a member's email:

- Number of dictations per day
- Mode used (Voice, Email, Super, etc.)
- Voice model and language model selected
- Duration of recordings (in seconds)
- App version and platform (macOS or Windows)

This data powers the **Usage** tab in your dashboard and helps you gauge adoption, identify power users, and plan seat purchases.

## What We Never Collect

Usage analytics is metadata only. It does not include:

- **Audio recordings** of dictations
- **Transcribed text** or any content from a member's dictation
- **Custom vocabulary, modes, or replacements** configured by the member
- **Keystrokes, screen content, or activity outside the Superwhisper app**

Audio and transcripts are processed according to the rules described in [Sensitive Data Best Practices](/security/sensitive-data). Usage analytics is a separate, metadata-only telemetry stream and is not affected by your model choices.

## Disabling Analytics

Owners can disable usage analytics at any time from the dashboard. When disabled, the analytics endpoint stops receiving events from member devices on their next app launch.

## Privacy & Compliance

Superwhisper processes usage analytics metadata under the terms of our [DPA](/security/compliance#sign-a-dpa-or-baa). The data isn't used to train AI models, and isn't shared with third parties beyond the sub-processors listed in the DPA.

For organizations under HIPAA, usage analytics metadata is not PHI. The optional toggle exists for organizations that prefer to minimize all telemetry.
