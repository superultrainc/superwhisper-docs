## Step-by-Step

<Steps>
    <Step title="Download the App">
        Get Superwhisper for MacOS from [Our Website](https://superwhisper.com/).
    </Step>

    <Step title="Access your License Settings">
    Once installed, open the MacOS app and [navigate to Settings](settings). At the bottom left, click the button labeled “Superwhisper” to enter your license details.

    <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/get-started/activate-mac-001.png"
    alt="Desktop Pro Activation Screen"
    />
    
    </Step>

  <Step title="License Activation">
    To complete activation, you'll need to copy the key from your iOS app. 
      1. On your iOS app, go to Settings > "Superwhisper Pro"
      2. Locate the "Reveal License Key"
      3. Tap it to reveal your key
      4. You can then type this into the "License Key" Box on the MacOS app. Be sure to keep the upper case lettering. 

    <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/get-started/activate-mac-002.png"
    alt="Desktop QR Code Activation Screen"
    />

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Didn't Get Your License Key?

If you paid for Superwhisper Pro but didn't receive a license key in your inbox:

1. Check your spam or promotions folder for an email from `superwhisper.com`
2. Confirm the email on your order matches the one you're checking
3. Still nothing? Email [support@superwhisper.com](mailto:support@superwhisper.com) with the email or receipt from your purchase and we'll resend it
