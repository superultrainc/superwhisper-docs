## One License, All Your Devices

Your Superwhisper Pro license works across all platforms:

- **Mac** - Unlimited Mac computers
- **Windows** - Unlimited Windows PCs
- **iPhone** - Your personal device
- **iPad** - Your personal device

<Note>
You don't need to buy separately for each platform. A single Pro license unlocks Superwhisper on all your devices.
</Note>

## Where to Buy

You can purchase Superwhisper Pro from:

1. **Superwhisper Website** (Recommended)
   - Visit [superwhisper.com](https://superwhisper.com)
   - Purchase provides a license key that works everywhere

   - Your license key is available in Settings > Superwhisper Pro > Reveal License Key
   - Use this key to activate on Mac and Windows

## FAQ

### Do I need separate licenses for Mac and iPhone?
No. One Pro license works on all platforms—Mac, Windows, iPhone, and iPad.

### Can I use my license on multiple Macs/PCs?
Yes. There's no device limit for Mac and Windows computers.

### I bought on iOS. How do I activate on Mac or Windows?
Open the iOS app, go to Settings > Superwhisper Pro > Reveal License Key. Enter this key in your Mac or Windows app.

### Do my custom modes sync between devices?
Currently, modes and vocabulary are stored locally on each device. Cloud sync is planned for a future update.

### What's the difference between App Store and website purchase?
Both give you the same Pro features. Website purchases provide a license key directly. App Store purchases also have a license key available in the iOS app settings.
